Challenge C2B — "Cindy's Clever Trick"   (75 pts)
=================================================

Cindy's former password was compromised, so she decided to add an !1 to it,
thinking it was smart.

  Target:  c2b_md5.txt   (an MD5 hash)
  Goal:    recover her NEW password and submit  DC256{<password>}

This one is about MUTATION (rules / hybrid). A plain wordlist won't match —
you have to take each candidate word and tack on the same characters Cindy did.
(See the "Dictionary + rules" idea from the slides.)

No spoilers here — figure out the base word and the mutation. Good luck!
